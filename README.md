# Stay gluten-free in South Korea

## General Advice
You can never be 100% sure that a dish is gluten-free around here. Even if a dish should be in theory, because all of its ingredients are gluten-free, there still is the possibility of cross-contamination during its preparation. That's also the reason why a "100% Gluten-free Food" list cannot be provided, and rather you'll see a "Likely Gluten-free Food" list on this page. Always double check!

Here are a few facts and ingredients you should watch out for during your stay:

- __Gochujang__ (red pepper paste)
    - contains gluten
    - everything that looks a bit _red_ possibly contains _Gochujang_ or was prepared with it (e.g. mixed, marinated)
    - is often used to enhance dishes, so if you are not sure, ask about it in advance
- __Ganjang__ (Soy Sauce)
    - contains gluten
    - everything that looks a bit _dark_ possibly contains _Soysauce_ or was prepared with it (e.g. mixed, marinated)
- __Doenjang__ (fermented soybean paste)
- __Samjang__ (dipping sauce often served with K-BBQ)
- __Jjajang__
- meat and vegetables in general are often marinated in one or multiple of these sauces

## Likely Gluten-free Food
- K-BBQ (_probably your most reliable option_)
- Samgyetang, Galbitang, Seollongtang, Gomtang
- Kimchi Jjigae, Sundubu Jjigae
- Kimbap
- Sushi
- Bokkeumbap
- Gukbap
- Bindaeduk
- Japchae
- Pho
